LUMEN EATS / RESTAURANT & COASTAL KITCHEN
Since 1982

Lumen Eats operates as a traditional Spanish coastal kitchen, focusing on regional gastronomy and direct product sourcing. For over 40 years, our culinary concept has centered around two pillars: uncompromised raw product quality and authentic Andalusian preparation techniques. The menu balance ranges from classic tapas like Jamón Ibérico and Patatas Bravas to key regional staples, including Paella Mixta, Gazpacho Andaluz, Garlic Chili Sea Crab (Al Ajillo), and premium Chuletón beef. No fusion, no compromise—just clean Spanish flavors driven by fresh seafood and heritage recipes.
